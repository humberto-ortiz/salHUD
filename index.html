Years of Potential Life Lost, Puerto Rico 2008

For all Causes of Death

Demo of salHUD, a heads-up display for public health data. Click on a municipality to display the Years of Potential Life Lost (YPLL) due to premature death among the residents of the indicated municipality by the selected cause of death. Municipalities are shaded with lighter colors representing lower YPLL, darker shades higher YPLL.

Full source code for the demo is available on github.

The salHUD demo is a collaboration between hackers from three UPR campuses for the Puerto Rico Tech Summit Hackathon. Participants are:
